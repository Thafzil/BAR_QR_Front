Barcode swap_horiz QRcode Generator & Decoder

Generates Barcodes and QR codes for the given text/s.

Decodes Barcodes and QR codes for the given generated image/s

The text can be provided by a simple text or using a CSV file.

This application featured with downading and sharing the generated image.

And there is an option to save the image to the database for your future reference
