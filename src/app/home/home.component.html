Barcode Generator

A barcode generator is an application that creates a barcode image for a specific text or upto 100 values in a CSV file. Barcodes are used in retail, inventory management, and other industries to quickly and accurately identify items and track them through various stages of production, distribution, and sales.

QR code Generator

A QR code generator is an application that creates Quick Response (QR) codes, which are two-dimensional barcodes that can be read using a smartphone camera. QR codes can store information such as website URLs, contact information, product details, and more.

Barcode Decoder

A barcode decoder is a tool that can read and interpret barcode symbols. Barcodes are widely used in various industries, such as retail, transportation, and logistics, to store and retrieve information quickly and accurately. This is an essential tool for applications that need to process barcode data, such as inventory management, order tracking, and product authentication.

QR code Decoder

A QR code decoder is a program that can read and interpret QR codes from an image. A QR code is a type of two-dimensional barcode that can store information such as URLs, text, phone numbers, and more. QR codes are commonly used in marketing, advertising, and ticketing applications, and can be read by a smartphone camera or a specialized QR code reader.
